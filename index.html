Penguin in the Closet

A boy comes home from school and finds his house is a big mess! Along with the toys, books, and clothes he left out, there are silly animals everywhere like a penguin in the closet and a walrus on the stairs! He knows his mother will be back soon and she will not be happy to see the house like this. He decides to pick up his things and one by one the animals start to disappear. Can he work fast enough to clean the house before his mother comes home? Enjoy this entertaining story about a boy who learns the importance of picking up his things!

About

Jeanna Dwyer

Jeanna Dwyer works with children of all ages as a pediatric physical therapist. She is married and the mother of two active, young kids. Her son has a talent for leaving toys where they do not belong. He inspired Jeanna to write A Penguin in the Closet when she stumbled upon a purple toy penguin on her bedroom floor. Jeanna grew up in Minnesota, the land of 10,000 lakes. Every summer she looks forward to running a 12 mile race around the lake where her family has had a cabin for 40 years! Since childhood, Jeanna has loved books that entertain, empower, and educate readers. She shares this passion with her own children and looks forward to sparking the curiosity of other young readers through her writing.
