Penguin in the Closet

A boy comes home from school and finds his house is a big mess! Along with the toys, books, and clothes he left out, there are silly animals everywhere like a penguin in the closet and a walrus on the stairs! He knows his mother will be back soon and she will not be happy to see the house like this. He decides to pick up his things and one by one the animals start to disappear. Can he work fast enough to clean the house before his mother comes home? Enjoy this entertaining story about a boy who learns the importance of picking up his things!
